About the G1 Ontario Test

The G1 test is the first step for new drivers in Ontario to obtain their driver's license. This written knowledge test assesses your understanding of Ontario's traffic laws, road signs, and safe driving practices. The test is divided into two main sections:

  • Rules of the Road: Questions about Ontario's traffic laws, safe driving practices, and handling various driving situations.
  • Road Signs: Questions about the meanings and implications of various road signs found in Ontario.

To pass the G1 test, you need to answer at least 16 out of 20 questions correctly in each section. Preparing thoroughly is crucial to ensure you understand the material and can confidently pass the test.

Our free G1 practice exams are designed to help you prepare for the actual test by providing a simulated testing experience. These practice exams cover a wide range of questions similar to those you will encounter on the real G1 test. By taking our practice exams, you can:

  • Familiarize yourself with the format and types of questions on the G1 test.
  • Identify areas where you need further study and improvement.
  • Build your confidence and reduce test-day anxiety.
